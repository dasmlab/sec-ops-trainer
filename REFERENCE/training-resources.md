# Training Resources

## Overview

This document provides a comprehensive list of training resources, links, and courseware for the SecOps Standards Officer competency development path. Resources are organized by domain area and tier.

---

## Training Philosophy

- **RHEL-First Approach**: Prioritizes Red Hat Enterprise Linux training
- **Practical Experience**: Emphasizes hands-on labs and real-world practice
- **Standards-Focused**: Integrates standards body training
- **Progressive Learning**: Resources aligned with tier progression

---

## Training Planning Tips

1. **Align with Tier**: Choose training appropriate for current tier
2. **RHEL-First**: Prioritize Red Hat training for RHEL-focused environments
3. **Certifications**: Align training with certification goals
4. **Practical Labs**: Ensure hands-on practice opportunities
5. **Standards Integration**: Include standards body training
6. **Balance**: Balance vendor-specific and vendor-neutral training
7. **Continuous Learning**: Plan ongoing training and education
8. **Budget**: Consider training budgets and priorities
